About

Countrymen Volleball at the SW Veterans Tournament in Exeter 2024

We are a local volleyball club that trains and plays every Sunday, at Cornwall College Sports Center, Camborne from 18:00 to 20:00.

Due to insurance players have to be over 16, but other than that, anyone is welcome here at our friendly club, male and female. Come and join us to keep fit, have fun, and play a great team sport.

We play at Camborne (and Ludgvan during exam times at the college).

We also play the annual South West Volleyball Veterans Tournament each year in November (over 35's) and the biennial Breizh-Volleyades Plounéour-Trez tournament in Brittany, France where teams from all over France (and us from Cornwall) attend for a week of volleyball and traditional Bretton food. When these tournaments aren't on we like to also attend tournaments in Salisbury and the Netherlands.

We are very much a social club as well as a volleyball club, enjoying a drink together and getting the extra day or two out of a tournament to make a trip of it. So if either, or both of those pique your interest, please get in contact and we look forward to playing Volleyball with you!
